About Me

My journey through education has been quite the ride! from grappling physics concepts to trying to conjugate Spanish verbs (yo hablo español muy bien, por supuesto), I thought I had seen it all. But then, I discovered the wonderful world of computer science at the Technological University of Dublin and boy, was I hooked! I learned all about the inner workings of machines, from data structures and algorithms to web development and software engineering. And let's not forget about the thrilling rollercoaster ride that was Object Oriented Programming - who knew you could have so much fun with classes and objects? Of course, I learned about computer architecture too, because what good is a shiny new program if it doesn't run smoothly on the hardware? All in all, my education has been a wild ride, but I wouldn't have it any other way!

My work experience has been just as thrilling! I had the opportunity to work at Ocuclo where I honed my skills in .NET, HTML, JS, jQuery, and various APIs like AJAX and C#. The work was challenging, but it pushed me to grow as a developer and to constantly learn new things. From developing user interfaces to designing databases and implementing complex functionality, I was able to immerse myself in the world of web development and software engineering.

Software development has been quite an adventure! From learning the basics of programming languages such as .NET, Java, C++ , C, Python, PHP, and more, to creating dynamic web applications using HTML, JS, jQuery and various API's such as Ajax, RESTful, and more, my experience has been both challenging and rewarding. I've worked with different types of databases such as SQL, PostgreSQL, MongoDB, and more, managing them using tools like pgAdmin. Each project has been a new opportunity to learn and grow, and I'm grateful for the chance to work with such a variety of technologies.

My skills and hobbies are diverse, but I'm particularly passionate about front-end web design and personal project development. I enjoy challenging myself through competitive coding on Google Kick Start and staying healthy with regular visits to the gym. I also love to explore new places and try different cuisines while traveling, as food is another one of my great passions.
